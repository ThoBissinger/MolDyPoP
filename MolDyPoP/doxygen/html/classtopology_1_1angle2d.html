Detailed Description

A (double-valued) angle in 2d space with the possibility of identifying it with its corresponding Vector2d unit vector. In the current state of the simulation, this is redundant.

Mostly incomplete and unnecessary. The idea was to have a specialized double-like class that can easily be converted to Vector2d and back for simplified calcultion. But the spin and orthospin functions defined for double -> Vector2d actually do the trick perfectly fine. Leaving this here for someone feeling a bit freaky.

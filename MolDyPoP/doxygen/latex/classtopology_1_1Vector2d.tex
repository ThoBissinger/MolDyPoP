G\+E\+T\+\_\+\+B\+O\+U\+N\+D\+A\+R\+Y\+\_\+\+H\+A\+N\+D\+L\+E\+R\+\_\+\+P\+E\+R\+I\+O\+D\+I\+C\+\_\+\+B\+OX\+: A boundary handler is a \mbox{\hyperlink{classtopology_1_1Vector2d}{Vector2d}} that determines whether the vector is close to the boundary of a periodic box.

\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A group of polar particles. Stores vectors with particle positions, velocities, spin orientations and spin rotation velocity, as well as further group properties. 

Contains the main data to be manipulated in a simulation of the MXY model and the other models.

Functionalities


\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item Constructors
\item Functions for clearing and initialization as well as handling the partition member variable
\item Operations for reading and copying from other groups
\item Printing operations
\item Simple information extraction
\item Simple arithmetic operations on individual particles and their properties (differences, scaling, setting to new values etc.)
\item Calculation of physical properties (kinetic temperature, energz, momentum, helicity etc.)
\item Calculation of field fluctuations
\item Calculation for spatial and temporal correlation functions as well as correlations in reciprocal space
\item Calculation of time derivatives
\end{DoxyItemize}

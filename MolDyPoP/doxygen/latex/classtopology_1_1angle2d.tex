\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A (double-\/valued) angle in 2d space with the possibility of identifying it with its corresponding \doxylink{classtopology_1_1_vector2d}{Vector2d} unit vector. In the current state of the simulation, this is redundant. 

Mostly incomplete and unnecessary. The idea was to have a specialized double-\/like class that can easily be converted to \doxylink{classtopology_1_1_vector2d}{Vector2d} and back for simplified calcultion. But the spin and orthospin functions defined for double -\/\texorpdfstring{$>$}{>} \doxylink{classtopology_1_1_vector2d}{Vector2d} actually do the trick perfectly fine. Leaving this here for someone feeling a bit freaky.
